About Us

Welcome to Digitech Surveillance Solutions, your trusted partner in advanced security and surveillance systems. Based at Shop No.6, UDA Complex, Moti Nagar, Unnao–209801, we are a leading service provider committed to delivering top-notch surveillance solutions with excellence, reliability, and unmatched support. At Digitech, we specialize in Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for a wide range of properties, including residential buildings, commercial complexes, industrial factories, retail shops, shopping malls, and hospitals. Whether it’s a small business or a large-scale enterprise, our expert team ensures smooth functioning, timely service, and long-term protection for all your surveillance systems. Our reputation is built on years of consistent performance, skilled technicians, and a customer-first approach. We offer installation, troubleshooting, and routine servicing of CCTV cameras, biometric systems, alarm systems, and other security devices. Clients rely on us not just for technical expertise, but for our commitment to maintaining high standards across every project we undertake. With 24/7 support, a proactive service model, and a strong belief in innovation, Digitech Surveillance Solutions continues to grow as a dependable name in the security industry. Your safety is our mission—and we deliver it with precision and pride.
